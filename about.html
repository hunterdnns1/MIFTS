About the Series

The Michigan Ice Fishing Tournament Series began four years ago with a simple goal: to bring together anglers from across the state to celebrate the thrill of ice fishing. What started as a friendly series of competitions has quickly evolved into Michigan’s premier ice fishing championship. Each year, anglers compete in multiple qualifying events, testing their skills on Michigan’s frozen waters. The top competitors from each region advance to the state championship, creating an exciting and competitive atmosphere that draws participants from all corners of the state. Our mission is to provide a fun, fair, and safe environment for ice fishing enthusiasts of all skill levels, while promoting the sport and fostering a strong community of anglers. Whether you’re a seasoned pro or new to hardwater fishing, the Michigan Ice Fishing Tournament Series is your chance to experience the best of the sport, compete for prizes, and connect with fellow anglers.
