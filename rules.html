2025 Tournament Rules

All participants must follow these rules to ensure a fair and safe tournament:

  • Each team may have a maximum of two anglers per qualifying event.
  • Only legal methods of ice fishing are permitted. No explosives or spears.
  • All fish must be weighed in at the official weigh-in stations.
  • Teams must check in at the designated time for each event; late arrivals may be disqualified.
  • Respect other anglers, property, and the environment at all times.
  • Any disputes will be resolved by the tournament officials, whose decisions are final.
  • All participants are responsible for their own safety on the ice.

By registering, you agree to follow all rules and regulations outlined for the Michigan Ice Fishing Tournament Series.
