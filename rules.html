Michigan Ice Fishing Tournament Series – Rules

Please read all rules carefully to ensure fair play and eligibility for all events.

Event Structure

  • There are 3 qualifying events: North, Central, and South.
  • The top 10 teams from each event advance to the State Championship.
  • 50% of buy-ins paid out at the qualifying event. 50% reserved for the State Championship payout.
  • Shotgun start at 7:30 AM, weigh in at 12:30 PM

Teams and Entry

  • Teams may consist of 1 or 2 anglers.
  • Entry Fee: $200 per team.
  • Big bluegill/crappie fee (optional): $20 per team.
  • Each angler must have a valid Michigan fishing license.
  • All entry fees must be submitted before the tournament begins.
  • Same day registration begins at 6:30 AM. If registering at the event, cash only.
  • Online registration will close 1 week prior to the event.
  • Teams must bring their fish bucket to registration and recieve a numbered tag for their bucket.
  • No refunds.

Fish and Limits

  • Only panfish (bluegill, sunfish, crappie) are eligible for weighing.
  • All fish must be caught legally during tournament times.
  • 10-fish limit per team. MAXIMUM OF 5 CRAPPIES.
  • No frozen, iced, or altered fish allowed.
  • Tournament director/staff reserves the right to refuse fish that do not follow the above rules.

Weigh In

  • Weigh-in opens at 12:30PM
  • Buckets must be brought directly from the lake to the designated weigh area.
  • Teams must have their buckets to the designated area BY 12:30PM or be disqualified.
  • Big bluegill, and big crappie must be tagged with the provided tag.
  • Once buckets have been placed in the designated area, teams will not be allowed to touch them until after tournament staff have weighed their fish.
  • Heaviest total weight wins.
  • Tiebreaker will be determined by heaviest single fish, longest single fish, highest number of bluegills, coin flip.

Safety

  • All registered anglers assume the risk that ice fishing presents.
  • Michigan Ice Fishing Tournament Series and its affiliates will not be held liable for any injury/death that occurs while traveling to or from an event, or at the event.
  • We do our best to provide a safe event, but cannot guarantee that the ice is 100% safe.
  • Particapte at your own risk.

By participating, you agree to all rules and acknowledge that tournament officials have final authority in all decisions.
